What is Isolation Forest Model

An isolation forest model is a type of machine learning model that is trained with data about the pattern over a large period of time. If it encounters any instance when behavior falls out of this pattern, it will be considered suspicious and flagged as an outlier.

Suppose you use online transactions to make payments. You usually do this within a certain time window and the amount is within a specific limit. One day, if you make a payment of a large amount or at an unusual time, your bank will send you an alert message or email about the transaction. This behavior is generally analyzed using the isolation forest model.


The isolation forest model uses existing data to predict anti-patterns, but it may not always be accurate. Therefore, it could be YOU making a payment in the middle of the night for an item you found on YouTube :-)


Anomaly detection identifies suspicious activity that falls outside of your established normal patterns of behavior
